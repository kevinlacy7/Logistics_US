## 📊 Overview

This project analyzes shipment data to identify **cost leakage, operational inefficiencies, and delay drivers** across carriers, lanes, regions, and equipment types.

The goal is to transition from reactive reporting → **proactive, data-driven logistics optimization** that improves **profitability and Revenue Per Mile (RPM)**.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

**Primary Question:**    
Where are delays, detentions, and inefficiencies driving increased costs and reduced Revenue Per Mile (RPM), and how can they be corrected?

### Key Goals:
- Reduce late delivery penalties and detention costs  
- Improve Revenue Per Mile (RPM)  
- Increase carrier and lane efficiency  
- Identify high-risk shipments before dispatch

## 💸 Financial Impact

### Cost Drivers

- 🔻 Late Deliveries → Penalties + lost contracts  
- ⏱️ Detention Time → Higher cost per load  
- 🚛 Deadhead Miles → Lower RPM  

### Cost Leakage Sources

- Underperforming carriers  
- Inefficient intermodal transfers  
- Poor long-haul planning  
- High-risk regions without contingency
